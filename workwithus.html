Work With Us

We are currently open for pitches for pamphlets of 4,000-10,000 words. We welcome submissions from anyone, but especially women, in particular women underrepresented in the literary world and political thought- single mothers, refugees, lesbians, care leavers, disabled women, and those without a university education, to name a few. We are open to any type of politically engaged nonfiction writing or multimedia work.

To get in touch with a pitch, email us at gauzebonnetpress@gmail.com
